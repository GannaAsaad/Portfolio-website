About me

Hi, my name is Gannatullah, and I am a dedicated data scientist with a passion for transforming data into meaningful insights. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Business Analytics from the Egyptian Russian University, graduating with a CGPA of 3.86.

With a strong foundation in tools like Python, R, and Power BI, I’m constantly learning and striving to turn challenges into actionable solutions.
